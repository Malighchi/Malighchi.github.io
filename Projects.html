Visible Light Communication


A circuit diagram of converting light to sound to light and back to sound.

A project demonstrating how to convert sound to light and back to sound. Using a simple circuit, an led, a solar cell, and a phone we transferred the sound into electromagnetic waves and back into sound through the solar cell which was connected to an amp; a full explanation and real world application can be found here.
